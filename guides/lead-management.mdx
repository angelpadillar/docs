# Lead Management

Learn how to effectively manage and organize your leads using Buena.ai's lead management system.

## Overview

Our lead management system helps you:
- Organize leads by status and priority
- Track lead interactions and history
- Set up automated follow-up workflows
- Integrate with your CRM systems

## Key Features

### Lead Organization
- Custom lead statuses
- Tags and categories
- Lead scoring
- Pipeline management

### Automation
- Automated lead assignment
- Follow-up reminders
- Lead nurturing sequences
- CRM synchronization

1. Import your existing leads
2. Set up your lead statuses
3. Configure automation rules
4. Start managing your pipeline
